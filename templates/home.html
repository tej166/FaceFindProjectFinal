Precautions During Disaster

  • Stay informed about the disaster and follow the advice of local authorities.
  • Prepare an emergency kit with essential supplies such as food, water, and first aid items.
  • Have a communication plan in place with family members and loved ones.
  • Evacuate to a safe location if instructed to do so by authorities.
  • Avoid flooded areas and areas prone to landslides or other hazards.
  • Stay calm and help others in need if it is safe to do so.

ABOUT US

Retrouver is a website that helps families find their loved ones during natural disasters. Our mission is to provide a platform where families can upload their loved ones' photos and use advanced algorithms to find similar profiles. We also provide general information about natural disasters and the precautions that need to be taken.
